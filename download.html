Download

Download Biscuit.

Already adopted? Grab the app below, then wake Biscuit with the license key from your receipt email.

Download for Apple Silicon

M1 or newer, macOS 12 Monterey or later. The download is a DMG.

After downloading

  1. Open the DMG and drag Biscuit into Applications.
  2. Open Biscuit and enter the license key from your receipt email. The same key works across your Macs, up to 5.
  3. Grant Accessibility permission so Biscuit can react to your typing. He knows a key was pressed, never which one.

Stuck, or can’t find your key? Email hekman.nathan+1@gmail.com and a human will sort it out.
